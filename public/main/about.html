About


Welcome to 'CurioLearn,' your gateway to a world of knowledge and exploration! CurioLearn is a full-stack web development project designed to make learning and discovery effortless and engaging. Imagine having an encyclopedia and YouTube combined, ready to quench your curiosity instantly.

With CurioLearn, you can type in any topic or question you're curious about, and it will swiftly provide you with a wealth of information and related videos. It's a one-stop platform that merges the power of the Wikipedia API and YouTube API, offering a seamless learning experience.

Our website offers user-friendly features like personalized profiles, favorite content saving, and progress tracking. You can bookmark articles, take interactive quizzes, and participate in community discussions. We've designed a user-centric platform that adapts to your learning preferences, whether you're a visual learner, an avid reader, or a hands-on enthusiast.

CurioLearn also embraces inclusivity, with multilingual support and accessible design. It encourages collaboration, allowing users to contribute their knowledge and insights.
